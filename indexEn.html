A piece of history

In the heart of the oldest city in Europe, a portal awaits you. A portal that gives you a chance to peek into the past and to celebrate the present. Located in the center of Plovdiv, within a walking distance of the main cultural and tourist attractions, Villa Flavia Boutique Hotel offers you unique atmosphere and exciting experiences.


The building carries the characteristics of typical early 20th century Plovdiv architecture, wrapped in the French secession.


The hotel’s uniqueness is complemented by its authentic spirit and attention to detail. The exquisite rooms and stylish suites offer a charming mixture of classic and modern furnishing, which combined with its modern amenities and refined luxuries will turn Villa Flavia Boutique Hotel into your new favorite place to relax and connect with history.

Survived to be seen

Remains of a Roman bath dating from the 1st-2nd century have been found in the foundations of the hotel. Well preserved and exhibited, they provide an exceptional view into the heart of the majestic and millennial city of Plovdiv.


Villa Flavia is not just a hotel – it is a home to history, culture and elegant design that will guide you on your way to experience the past and celebrate the future!
